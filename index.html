The Sunday Night Vault

It is 8pm on a Sunday. The week ahead is locked, and three locks stand between you and it. Work through them in order. Each one gives you a word.

Before you start

  • Set aside 90 minutes. You can stop and come back, but do it on the same computer and browser or your work will not follow you.
  • Use a computer, not a phone. The planner in Lock 2 is a full seven day grid and will not fit on a phone screen.
  • Keep a scrap of paper next to you. Write down each word as you collect it.
  • Do it Sunday afternoon if you can. The whole thing is built around the week you are about to walk into.
